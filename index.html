About Arctic SMP

Arctic SMP is a community-driven Minecraft server focused on providing a chill and friendly environment for players of all ages. Join us to explore, build, and survive in a unique Arctic-themed world!

Server Rules

  • No griefing or stealing.
  • Be respectful to other players.
  • No hacks or cheats.
  • Keep chat friendly and appropriate.
  • Have fun and enjoy the game!

Server Features

Explore custom biomes, participate in community events, and enjoy a player-driven economy. Arctic SMP offers a rich gameplay experience that includes:

  • Player Shops and Economy
  • Custom Biomes and Structures
  • Weekly Events and Challenges
  • Community Builds
